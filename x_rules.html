01
General Conduct
1.1
Respect All Members
Treat every clan member with respect regardless of rank or skill level. Harassment, bullying, or targeted disrespect will not be tolerated.
Warning → Kick
1.2
No Toxicity
Excessive trash talk, rage, or toxic behaviour in clan activities or the server is forbidden. Keep it competitive, not personal.
Warning → Kick
1.3
No Discrimination
Zero tolerance for racism, sexism, or any form of discrimination. Any slur or targeted hate = instant ban, no appeal.
Instant Permanent Ban
02
Activity Requirements
2.1
Minimum Weekly Activity
All members must be active at least 3 times per week. Inactive members with no notice will be removed after 7 days.
Removal After 7 Days
2.2
LOA (Leave of Absence)
If you need time off, DM an admin before going inactive. An approved LOA protects your spot in the clan.
2.3
Clan Events
Attendance at official clan events and wars is mandatory for Main Lineup members. Missing 3 events without notice = demotion.
Demotion
03
Gameplay & Fair Play
3.1
No Cheating or Exploiting
Using hacks, exploits, or any unauthorised third-party tools in any game mode is strictly forbidden.
Instant Permanent Ban
3.2
Represent the Clan Properly
When playing under the Wraith tag, your behaviour reflects the whole clan. Poor sportsmanship in public lobbies reflects on us all.
Warning → Kick
3.3
No Kill Farming Between Members
Artificially boosting your kill count by farming other Wraith members is not allowed. Stats must be legitimate.
Kick + Stat Reset
3.4
Tryout Results are Final
All tryout accept and decline decisions made by admins are final. Do not reapply if declined. Doing so results in a permanent ban.
Permanent Ban
04
Server & Communication
4.1
Stay On Topic
Use the correct channels for their intended purpose. Spamming or going off-topic in lineup or announcement channels is not allowed.
Warning
4.2
No Spam or Flooding
Sending repeated messages, mass pinging, or flooding any channel is forbidden.
Kick
4.3
No NSFW Content
Sharing inappropriate, graphic, or NSFW content anywhere on this platform results in an immediate ban.
Instant Permanent Ban
4.4
Respect Admin Decisions
Do not argue or undermine admin decisions publicly. If you have a dispute, DM an admin privately and respectfully.
Warning → Kick
05
Ranks & Promotions
5.1
Rank Hierarchy
Ranks in order: Admin → Elite → Main Lineup → Sub → Recruit. Each rank comes with different privileges and responsibilities.
5.2
Earning Promotions
Promotions are based on kills, activity, attitude, and event performance. You cannot request a promotion — admins decide.
5.3
Demotions
Breaking rules, going inactive, or poor conduct can result in demotion. Repeated demotions result in removal.
Demotion
5.4
Impersonating Ranks
Pretending to be an admin or higher rank than you are is an instant permanent ban.
Instant Permanent Ban
